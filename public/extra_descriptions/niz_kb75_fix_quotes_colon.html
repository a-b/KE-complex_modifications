Plum Nano 75 Bluetooth NiZ KB75 75EC-XRGB (fix quotes and semicolon)

This keyboard was first available under the brand name Plum Nano 75 and later changed to NiZ KB75 75EC-XRGB. It identifies its vendor as CATEX TECH.

The firmware has a bug with switched keys. Pressing the results in a : on screen. The keyboard actually sends + to the computer. Consequently you have to press + to get a ;. The same applies for and .

This modification fixes this to work like most normal keyboards.

Key Press Character
;
+ :
'
+ "
